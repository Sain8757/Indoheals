Shipping & Delivery

Orders are typically processed within 24 hours. Delivery takes 3-5 business days for metro cities and 5-7 business days for other locations across India.

We offer free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999. For orders below this amount, a nominal shipping fee of ₹50 is applied.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your order directly from the "My Orders" section in your account.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery, provided the products are unopened, unused, and in their original packaging. For hygiene reasons, opened health supplements cannot be returned.

To initiate a return, go to "My Orders", select the item you wish to return, and click on "Request Return". Our delivery partner will pick up the item within 2-3 business days.
